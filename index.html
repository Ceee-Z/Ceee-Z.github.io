Restaurant centennial
Silvio Coppola's project for the El Prospero restaurant in the mid-60s, is a very classic design combining architecture, product, graphic and exhibition. A series of posters of this work are included in the Graphics Project's AIAP Historical Archives.

Silvio Coppola designed a first poster series in which the restaurant identity was for the first time associated with an apple, symbolizing the temptation offered by El Prospero‘s cuisine.
